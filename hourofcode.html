About Hour of Code

One of our significant jobs is participating in the Hour of Code event. Concisely, people globally are encouraged to have an hour-long lesson at an educational institution, and our mission is to provide information regarding computer science and how essential is coding to students.
ALTF4 team has been debating about the roles each member takes for making the lesson comprehensible and engrossing. Additionally, we are striving to achieve our goal of helping other learners to imagine their bright future careers during the presentation. Eventually, after searching for a school, our decision fell on School №220.
